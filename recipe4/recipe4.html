Lamb Curry



India


Absolutely outstanding and fragrant, home cooked comfort food.Going to my partner's mum's house is always a massive treat for me. As soon as the door opens, her sweet smile greets and the powerful aroma of all the indian spices you could ever think of lures you in. You've not experienced proper Indian food unless you've been lucky enough to taste authentic, homecooked Indian 'Mum' food.

Ingredients

Method

Step 1

In a large deep pan, add the oil/ ghee, and whole spices. Finally chop the onion and saute until almost brown

Step 2

Next, add the ginger and garlic and cook for another minute. Add the lamb, sear with the whole spices and onions for a few minutes until there is no pink is left in the meat.

Step 3

Now add the ground spices (except for the garam masala), salt and pepper, green chillis, and tinned tomatoes and bring to the boil. Lower temperature to a medium-low heat and let simmer.

Step 4

Continue to let it cook through for an hour and a half until the meat becomes tender, adding water now and again if necessary. Add the garam masala and simmer for a further few minutes. Once cooked, add lots of coriandar to finish.

Step 5

Serve with fluffy rice, naan, or roti.
